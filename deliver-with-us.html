--- layout: default title: Why Deliver With Thingogo? ---


Earn more when you deliver with Thingogo


Turn your vehicle into a money-making machine. The city is buzzing with a growing number of online shoppers, and the demand for deliveries has never been higher. Thingogo makes it easy for you to make good money by performing simple deliveries.




Flexible delivery timing and location


Busy during the day? No problem! As a Thingogo driver, you’ve got the freedom and flexibility to set your own schedule, deliver anytime from 10am to 9pm island-wide. There is no need to deliver all across the country, drivers can choose their delivery location cluster which they feel most comfortable at.



Simple pick-up and delivery process


Pick up parcels from our strategically placed warehouses. Parcels are then scanned and automatically updated in the Driver app. That’s it, load up your vehicle and you’re ready to go.



Seamless user experience


The app is incredibly easy to use. No complicated functions, no convoluted designs, simply register/sign in and you’re ready to perform deliveries.
