WE KNOW CAROUSELL


Have you ever feel it's hard to set up a convenience meet-up with your buyers/sellers? If the answer is yes, we are with you. We, the very first members of Thingogo, are all active Carousell users for years.

We deliver your item from MRT to MRT at a fixed rate and does not matter how far it is. Let us make your next delivery. At your convenience place. At your convenience time.



STEP 1: CAROUSELL

Nothing is new here. Find your sellers/buyers as you do everyday.




STEP 2: WHATSAPP 9866-9351

Create a Whatsapp group for 3: seller, buyer and Thingogo. Feel free to add us at 9866 9351 to discuss meetup MRT and timing.




STEP 3: MEET US

Bring your item to your convenience MRT. Usually we will deliver immediately, it takes upto 2 hour. We can store the item and deliver on the next day according to your request.




FINAL STEP: GET MONEY

All update will be made via Whatsapp, we make it simple, friendly yet secure. Once delivered, you will get money to your bank acc in an hour.




JUST IN CASE

Buyer doesn't show up? Buyer refuse to buy? The item will get back to you. No additional charge.
